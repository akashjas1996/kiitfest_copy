Power Relay

Description

Power relay is about having knowledge about basic electrical components and circuit design.Showcase your skills with your ideas about electrical things. Design the best circuit you do with least time. What matters is knowledge and speed with accurate result.So, Think and design how fast and how accurate you can be!

Rounds

ROUND 1(QUIZ TEST): In this round, all the participants will be giving a MCQ test based on the basics about relays. Top 10 teams will be selected for the next round.
ROUND 2(ERROR CORRECTION): In this round, a wrong circuit will be provided to all the teams. Top 5 teams to correct the circuit in minimum time will be selected for next final round.
ROUND 3(CIRCUIT COMPLETION): In this final round, all the teams will be provided with equipment’s and circuit diagram. Team can select equipment only once at the beginning and have to use all. Top 2 teams to complete within least time will be decided by the judges as winner.

Judging Criteria

1. 1st round will be judged on the teams who will score the maximum in MCQ. Top 10 accurate teams will be further selected for next round. 2. 2nd round will be based on basic knowledge skills with speed and accuracy. Top 5 teams will be further selected for final round. 3. 3rd round will be based on visual, technical and accuracy skills. Final round will be judged with respect to accuracy and speed.

Robo Soccer

Description

Design a manually controlled mechanical bot, which can push or kick a medium sized ball. The prime motive of this game is to put the ball on your opponent’s goal post and save from yours. The number of goals made by each bot will be counted and highest goal will be the winner.

Specification

TEAM:- In each team there should be only two members who will be playing with the bot. Each team should have every necessary item which is to be needed for there bot to be played.
BOT:- The bot shall be manually controlled and can be wired or wireless. The potential difference between any two points should not exceed 24V.
i. Maximum size of the bot should not exceed 30cm*30cm*30cm (Strictly to be followed).
ii. Bot weight should not exceed 3.5kg.
iii. Expansion of the bot during the match is not allowed (Strictly to be followed).
iv. Bot should be made properly, so that there should not be any harm in the Arena.
v. No sticking or ball holding mechanism should not be present in the bot. Bot should only push or kick the ball (Strictly to be followed).
vi. Power relay mechanism should not be used in connection as well as no mechanical gripers or Lego should be attached in the bots (Strictly to be followed).
vii. The power supplies for the bot can be on-board or off-board.
BALL:- Ball diameter: - 6-7 cm. The ball may be of plastic or rubber or tennis ball

Gameplay

FORMAT: Each goal scored will give 200 points. In each of team makes goal for three times continuously then that will get a free hit and score 300 points at a time adding with the point of each goal he secured. There will be three rounds in the event:-

Round 1: Qualifiers
i. In this round, each team will try to shoot goals from 5 fixed locations of the arena. Single attempt will be allowed from each location. This round will last for 5 minutes.
ii. The bots should not move more than 25cm from the battle area after taking a shot.
iii. Maximum goals to be made within the stipulated time are to be maintained.
iv. Accordingly, rank will be given, and they will be moved to next round. Knock out
In this round the tie will be broke down and they will be moved to next round.
Round 2: SEMI FINALS
i. The selected teams will be played within themselves further.
ii. Here also time limit is 10 minutes and there will be two halves the round will be performed.
iii. Again, the winners will be moved to finals.
Round 3: FINALS
i. Each match will be continued for 20 minutes and there will be two halves the round will be performed.
ii. Each second saved from the limit of 5 minutes will give 1 point each. Gross point is calculated.
iii. In case of tie in ranking, the team with maximum number of goals will be given priority.

General Guidelines

i. The bot is not allowed to leave anything behind during the match. Any bot found damaging the arena will be immediately disqualified.
ii. The score calculated by the organizers will be final and will be used for scoring the teams.
iii. In case of any disputes/discrepancies, the organizers’ decision will be final and binding. The organizers reserve the right to change any of the above rules as they deem fit. Changes in rules, if any will be highlighted on the website, social networking sites and notified to the teams.
iv. The registered teams must reach the arena in the specified time.

Other Guidelines

i. The team with maximum number of goals after full time will be declared winner.
ii. In case of a tie in score, 3 Free kicks at different positions shall be given to both the teams. The team scoring maximum no. of goals will be declared winner.
iii. The bot must not enter the opponent's D- Box otherwise penalty shot will be given to the opponent team.
iv. If at any point during the match the two bots gets stuck and are not able to move then the match will be paused and resumed from the nearest neutral point.

Guidelines for Round 1

Video Presentation:
The Video Option represents a new opportunity to communicate your scholarly research - in addition to the existing Performance of electrical engineering and technology. These videos should be short presentations (2 minutes or less of content, plus up to 30 seconds of acknowledgments) that capture the excitement, importance, and value of your scholarly work or views. They will be judged using criteria appropriate to the video format and goals of this exhibition. Specific judging criteria are provided below. Judging Criteria for the Video Presentation:
1. Content (10 pts):
i. Purpose of Research (10 pts.) – The “What?”
ii. Background, current state of knowledge (10 pts.) – The “What?”
iii. How this will be done (10 pts.) – The “How?”
iv. Potential Impact of Research (10 pts.) – The “Why?”
2. Effectiveness (10 pts) - successful communication
i. Information is accessible to a broad audience (10 pts.)
ii. Key concepts are clearly communicated (10 pts.)
iii. Innovative and creative use of video format (10 pts.)
3. Quality (10 pts.)
i. Time constraints are met and effectively used (10 pts.)
ii. Visuals are clear, effective, and appropriate for topic (10 pts.)
iii. Text, music, narration is clear and appropriate for video (10 pts.)
Participants who have qualified through Round 1 will be invited to give an 8 minute presentation on Technical Experiences and Innovative Ideas in round 2

iv. Slide No.4 – Any alternate methods.
