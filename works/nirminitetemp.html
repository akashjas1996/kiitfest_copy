Bridge It

This is an event of civil engineering which made bridge designing a reality by letting builders design and make a real model bridge that was to be tested according to the given conditions.

Team Criteria

1. Group participation, maximum member allowed is 3.
2. Solo participation is allowed

Materials

1) Popsicle Sticks/ Ice Cream Sticks
2) Sticks can be physically altered in any way including: -
i. It can be cut at any angle
ii. Sanded to any width
iii. Stick must not be soaked in any material except water
iv. Sticks must not be painted or coated with any materials
v. Only Fevicol should be used for sticking as an adhesive

Design Specification

1. Maximum Span = 75 cm
2. Maximum height = 30 cm
3. Maximum Weight = 350 grams
4. Max width = Between 10 -13 cm
5. Tolerance in height/width/span = +/- 2 cm
6. Tolerance in Weight = +/- 10 gram

Those who do not follow the rules will be disqualified at any cost. The final decision will be taken by judging panel.
Note: -
1. Arch bridge would not be entertained.
2. Complete overlapping of sticks is not allowed.

Judging Criteria

1. Efficiency of bridge
2. Aesthetic

Card-o-Holics

Models illustrate the different kinds of materials and various styles of architecture used in civil engineering over centuries. It is a miniature representation of structures, both natural and man-made, that depicts its form, style and pattern. This time is providing you with an opportunity to construct any Historic Monument and bring out the creative side in you. So, be a creator!! Be a real engineer!!
The task for the participants will have to make miniature replica models of Historic Monuments from all over the world or will have to make some ‘green building structure’ or will have to make his/her dream buildings with the help of Playing cards, or Thermocol or Popsicle sticks. You can use any type of adhesive. The models will be exhibited at the fest and will be evaluated by the judges. They will have to make the structure in their respective hostel or in home as it is an offline event and they will have to submit their work for exhibition within the given deadline.

Team Criteria

1. Maximum team size 4
2. Minimum 2

Rules

Maximum Weight: 3 Kg.
Event is open to all.
Note: -
1. Toothpicks can be used for fitting Thermocol.
2. Acrylic colour can be used for painting.
3. Glitter can be used for providing sparkling
4. Candle can be used to make columns of buildings
5. Cards must be cut at any angle

Judging Criteria

1. Aesthetic
2. Clarity
